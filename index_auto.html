About Craniosacral Therapy

Craniosacral Therapy enhances the body's innate ability to heal by benefiting organs, tissues, fasciae and body's numerous systems like the musculoskeletal, nervous, cardio-vascular, immune system etc. It helps to release deep-seated stress, injury, and trauma. It is safe and effective for people of all ages, from newborns to the elderly.

You remain fully clothed and relax on a comfortable, cushion-padded table. Using a gentle touch, the therapist evaluates and listens to the body for tension patterns and gently corrects them. A typical session lasts from 45 to 60 minutes and takes place in a quiet, clean, private & safe setting.

  • Reduction in chronic pain and stress
  • Improved sleep quality and better digestion
  • Relief from migraine-like headaches
  • Helps with body pain and arthritis
  • Greater sense of calm and well-being

For safety reasons, any individuals who are currently with severe conditions of any type must first be given proper medical care. Craniosacral Therapy (CST) is a gentle and widely safe modality, but it is important to note that it is intended to be a complementary therapy, not a substitute for conventional medicine.
